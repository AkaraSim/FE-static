Faculty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering of the Royal University of Phnom Penh was established in 2013 with the vision of training students in the field of engineering to be highly capable, innovative ideas with ethical research to meet the needs and help develop current society and globalization.

Environmental Engineering

Environmental Engineering dedicated to fostering human resources with higher education in Cambodia by providing students with a broad range of coursework in environmental engineering..

Department Automation & Supply Chain Systems

Automation and Supply Chain Systems is a branch of Engineering that deals with automation and digital manufacturing.

Department Of IT Engineering

Information engineering, also known as information technology engineering, information engineering methodology or data engineering, is a software engineering approach to designing and developing information systems.

Department Of Bio Engineering

Bio-Engineering/Biotechnology is a branch of engineering that deals with chemical and biological processes used to develop and make products and practices used in Medical and Health application, Food and Agro industry, Agriculture and Environment.

Department Of TE Engineering

Telecommunication and Electronics Engineering integrates two technologies including Telecommunication technology and Electronics technology.
