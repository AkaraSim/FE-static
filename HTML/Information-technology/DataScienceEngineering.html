Data Science & Engineering

Bachelor of Data Science and Engineering program at Faculty of Engineering, Royal University of Phnom Penh is intended to give students a top-notch education in the art of extracting value from data assets so that we can better understand the past, manage the present, and effectively plan for the future.

Department Vision

Bachelor in Engineering Program in Data Science and Engineering aims to produce graduates who are competent and ready to work in the 21st century, by developing soft skill: creativity, communication, teamwork, (Engineering Reasoning & Problem Solving) and self-learning process with knowledge of Data (Science & Engineering) and Artificial Intelligence

Job Prospects

Data Analyst

Data Analyst will process in inspecting, cleaning, transforming, and modeling data with the goal of discovering useful information, informing conclusions, and supporting decision-making

Business Intelligent Analyst

Business Intelligent Analyst gathers, analyzes, and compiles data needed to identify trends and patterns, then make recommendations for business actions

Associate Data Engineer

Implement data flows and set up automation between various data sources. Maintain data documentation and definitions. Help in building large-scale batch and real-time data pipelines with data processing frameworks

Data Engineer

Data engineers work in a variety of settings to build systems that collect, manage, and convert raw data into usable information for data scientists and business analysts to interpret. Their ultimate goal is to make data accessible so that organizations can use it to evaluate and optimize their performance

AI/ML Engineer

focuses on researching, building and designing self-running artificial intelligence (AI) systems to automate predictive models. Machine learning engineers design and create the AI algorithms capable of learning and making predictions that define machine learning (ML)

Event & Activity

Based on knowledges which are provided by IT Engineering department, graduates can work as Designer/Developer of Computer & Mobile Software, Database System Analyst/Developer, Network Engineer, and Game Developer.
